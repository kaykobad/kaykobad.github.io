Hi, I am Md Kaykobad Reza, a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at UC Riverside, advised by professor Dr. M. Salman Asif. I am from Bangladesh and I completed my B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.

My research interests broadly span the area of multimodal machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. I am currently focusing on enahncing robustness of multimodal machine learning models by designing/adapting models and fusion strategies that are computationaly efficient, can learn from nosiy/poor data and robust to missing modality scenarios.

I feel extremely privileged to have had Dr. M Sohel Rahman as my mentor, guiding me into the fascinating world of research and imparting comprehensive knowledge that has paved the way for my current position.

In the past, I was fortunate to have worked at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh and bKash Limited.
