Greetings,
I am Md Kaykobad Reza, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Riverside. I am working under the supervision of Professor Dr. M. Salman Asif. I completed my B.Sc. from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology and M.Sc. from University of California, Riverside. I am deeply honored to have had Dr. M Sohel Rahman as my mentor. His guidance introduced me to the fascinating world of research and imparted comprehensive knowledge that has laid the foundation for my current academic position.

My research interests broadly span the areas of multimodal machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and data mining. Currently, I am focusing on enhancing the robustness of multimodal machine learning models. This involves designing and adapting models and fusion strategies with specific goals in mind. Firstly, these models should be computationally efficient. Secondly, they must be capable of learning from noisy or poor-quality data. Lastly, they need to demonstrate resilience in scenarios involving missing, noisy, occluded, and unaligned modalities.

Prior to my current academic pursuit, I had the privilege of gaining valuable professional experience at Amazon, bKash Limited and Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh.
